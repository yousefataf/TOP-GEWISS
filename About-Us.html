ABOUT US

Founded in 2020 at a time when the world was facing great challenges but with our determination and persistence, we were able to continue working, producing and even excelling in the field of manufacturing U-PVC Rigid Conduits & Fittings, U-PVC Circular Boxes , U-PVC Flexible Conduits , Waterproof Junction Boxes and PE Pliable Conduits .

Our industrial approach relies on the use of high-purity raw materials supported by precise manufacturing standards, ensuring a final product of superior quality and stable performance that meets market requirements and complies with approved technical specifications.

We are proud of our products which are Water-proof, Dust-proof, and Fire-proof.
We are constantly developing and improving our machines and products to provide the best solutions on the market to our customers.

Our Vision

As part of our ongoing commitment to deliver reliable and highly efficient solutions in the electrical installation sector, we continuously develop advanced products engineered to improve power transmission efficiency and ensure the protection of facilities across diverse operational environments.
Our products are designed to compete directly with leading global brands in terms of performance, reliability, and cost-effectiveness, while maintaining full compliance with international technical standards and specifications.
This strategic approach is integral to our vision of establishing an advanced industrial framework capable of meeting both local and global market demands with exceptional efficiency and consistent quality.
