ABOUT US

Founded in 2020 at a time when the world was facing
great challenges but with our determination and persistence,
we were able to continue working, producing and even excelling
in the field of manufacturing U-PVC Rigid Conduits & Fittings,
U-PVC Circular Boxes , U-PVC Flexible Conduits , Waterproof Junction Boxes and PE Pliable Conduits.
We use the purest and finest materials to come up with
a high quality final product.
We are proud of our products which are Water-proof.
Dust-proof, and Fire-proof.
We are constantly developing and improving our
machines and products to provide the best solutions
on the market to our customers.
